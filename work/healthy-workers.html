A healthy business starts with Healthy Workers

Project Overview
Healthy Workers creates the best work environment to optimise employee engagement an productivity. Healthy Workers combines environmental data with science-based people analytics, to uncover actionable insights. This makes it possible for companies to invest in the best interventions that suit their employees.
My Contributions
Healthy Workers is a startup but in design wise pretty mature. In March 2019 I started here as Lead Product Designer. One of my first tasks was start building an app which focuses on the wellbeing of employees. For the first time in my working career i collaborated with external and internal developers in the same.

After finalizing the non-native app, our brand established a new identity. Therefore, we had to implement our existing functionality to our new brand identity. In sum, we have built a brand new app, corporate website and manager dashboard.

In Healthy Workers, Component Based Design is the practice of splitting UI into smaller, more manageable parts with clear names.

About the company Healthy Workers is a startup focused on improving work environments to optimise employee engagement and productivity. By combining environmental data with science-based people analytics we uncover actionable insights for office managers. This makes it possible for companies to invest in the best interventions that suit their employees.
‍Managers DashboardResponsive web dashboard for managers to analyse employees’ feedback and review sensor data for their building (temperature, humidity, air quality, etc.).
